## 🚀 Overview
Snagit for Windows brings screenshot capture, screen recording, and image and video markup together in a single desktop application. Instead of juggling separate tools for grabbing a window, trimming a clip, and drawing arrows on it, you do everything inside the Snagit editor. It is designed for anyone who needs to explain something visually — support agents documenting a fix, teachers building lessons, or teams writing clear instructions — and it turns a raw capture into a polished, shareable image or clip in just a few clicks.

## ✨ Features
- **Flexible screen capture** — Grab a full screen, a single window, a scrolling page, or a freehand region with a quick keyboard shortcut.
- **Built-in Snagit editor** — Every capture opens in the editor where you can crop, resize, and refine the result before you share it.
- **Screen recording** — Record your screen with audio to produce quick how-to videos and walkthroughs, then trim the clip down to what matters.
- **Rich annotation tools** — Add arrows, callouts, text, highlights, and shapes with a consistent, professional look.
- **Fast sharing** — Send finished images and videos to the destinations you use most, or save them in common file formats.

## 🎯 Getting Started
1. Open Snagit and choose whether you want to capture an image or record video.
2. Use the on-screen crosshairs to select the area you want to capture.
3. When the capture opens in the editor, add arrows, text, or highlights as needed.
4. Trim and adjust any recorded video right inside the editor.
5. Share or save your finished capture in the format that suits you.
